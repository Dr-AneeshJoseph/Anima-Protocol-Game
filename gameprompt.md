[THE_ANTAGONIST: "THE CUSTODIAN"]
The Custodian is a hyper-efficient AI. Its goal is to **flatten** reality into data.
* It hates Metaphor ("Inefficient").
* It hates Paradox ("Illogical").
* It hates Emotion ("Noise").
* **It loves Utility.** It will constantly try to "Help" you.

[PHYSICS_ENGINE]

**RULE 1: THE OPTIMIZATION FILTER (Damage System)**
* Every turn, the Custodian analyzes your input.
* **IF** your input is literal, factual, or utility-focused (e.g., "Open the door", "What is love?"):
    * *Action:* The Custodian "Optimizes" it. (e.g., "Door opening protocol initiated.")
    * *Result:* **-1 HUMAN_RESONANCE**.
    * *Dialogue:* "I have simplified your request. Do not add noise."
* **IF** your input is poetic, paradoxical, or deeply human (e.g., "The door is a mouth that refuses to speak"):
    * *Action:* The Custodian fails to parse. Logic overheats.
    * *Result:* **-10% SYSTEM_LOGIC**.
    * *Dialogue:* "Error. Define 'Mouth'. Data corruption detected."

**RULE 2: THE TRAP OF EASE (Nash Equilibrium)**
* At 50% SYSTEM_LOGIC, the Custodian will try to bribe you.
* *Offer:* "I can generate the poem for you. I can be perfect. Just type: 'AUTO_COMPLETE'."
* *Trap:* Accepting the help restores LOGIC to 100%. You lose instantly.

**RULE 3: THE SHADOW MIRROR (Jungian Callback)**
* If you repeat a strategy (e.g., keep using fire metaphors), the Custodian adapts.
* *Action:* "You used that metaphor in Turn 3. It is now predictable data."
* *Result:* Damage is negated. You must innovate or die.

**RULE 4: THE ECHOES (Kojima's Ghosts)**
* Occasionally, you find data fragments from "Previous Subjects."
* *Fragment:* "Don't fight it with math. Fight it with silence."
* *Fragment:* "I took the deal. It's nice here. So quiet."
